About Me

Who Am I?

My name is Sean Baskin and I am in my prime at twenty-nine (29) years old. I currently reside in the beautiful city of Chattanooga, Tennessee.

Sabbatical

There are a litany of reasons for why I departed my previous position and for why these many months later I have not transitioned to a new one, but I believe a "sabbatical" is the closest term to accurately describe them.

The original impetus for departing can be summarized as follows:

  • The company was downsizing and I was on the chopping block,
  • After numerous departures within the organization I was no longer on the chopping block, but this was temporary and I could potentially be re-visiting this exact scenario in three-to-six months time depending on future business development,
  • I had already begun looking for a new place to land,
  • and most importantly I had reached a point of isolation and burnout that was not sustainable.

Following my departure and a brief period of rest and relaxation, I succumbed to the trials and tribulations of no longer having the structure that defined my professional life. I had little interest in transitioning to a new job where I would be utilizing my skills in the same manner and environment from which I had departed. I had lost the passion for software development that had spurred my introduction and zeal for this field, and so began the process of finding that passion again, deep knowledge acquisition on an eclectic range of subject matter, and making a determination on the type of place I wanted to land next.
